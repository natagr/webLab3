Our History

2010

Our journey began in a quaint garage in the suburbs. The founder, Emily Thompson, had a passion for flora and a vision: to make fresh flowers an everyday luxury rather than an occasional indulgence. With limited resources but abundant dedication, Emily started creating bespoke floral arrangements for friends and family.

2017

As word spread, our brand grew. By 2017, demand had surged, and it was time for a change. We opened our first official storefront in the heart of the city. With a focus on sustainability and community, we sourced local flowers and provided workshops for budding florists. The store quickly became a hub for flower enthusiasts.

2022

Recognizing the digital shift, we took a bold step and launched our online store. This allowed us to extend our reach, delivering fresh bouquets nationwide. Our online platform not only showcased our diverse flower arrangements but also offered tips, care guidelines, and the rich history behind each bloom. Our commitment to quality and the digital experience made us a favorite among a new generation of flower lovers.
